Welcome. We are so happy to have you as part of the LIVESTRONG family. Hearing the words “you have cancer” can be devastating. It’s common to feel anger, sadness and disbelief. Here at LIVESTRONG, we believe that unity is strength, and we want you to know that e are here to support you every step of the way. You are not alone on your cancer journey.
